Question 2

What are the signs of fresh fish?

Clear, Bright Eyes:

Fresh fish have clear, bright, and slightly bulging eyes. The eyes should not appear cloudy or sunken.

Moist and Shiny Skin:

The fish's skin should be moist, shiny, and glistening. It should not be dry, dull, or discolored.

Firm Flesh:

press the flesh with your finger; it should feel firm and spring back. Avoid fish with soft or mushy flesh.

Ocean-Like Smell:

Fresh fish should have a mild, clean, and slightly briny odor reminiscent of the ocean. It should not have a strong or unpleasant fishy smell.

Red Gills (for some species):

If the fish has gills, they should be red or pink. Avoid fish with gray or brown gills. Clean and Unblemished Scales: If the fish has scales, they should be intact and shiny. There should be no missing or damaged scales.
